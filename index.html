ABOUT

Hi, my name is Alisha Gomes! I am a first-generation college student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology studying Information Systems, pursuing a career in information security. I am currently learning about cyber threat intelligence and penetration testing (you can keep up with what I am currently up to on Medium). 

My philosophy is to live with the end in mind (sounds bleak, I know), and one of my goals is to the world a more secure place by stopping adversaries in their tracks. Outside of school, I try to learn as much as possible about this field, and when I am reading or watching something interesting, I lose track of time. So whenever someone asks me why I chose cyber, I tell them it is because I get in this flow :). By giving my all into this work, I hope also to encourage others like me (women, people of color) who may not feel like they have a place in security to know that they do.
